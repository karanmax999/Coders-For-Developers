Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our platform and services.

What is CFD?

CFD (Coders for Developers) is a premier platform that connects elite developers with top companies and projects worldwide. We provide a curated network of verified talent, making it easier for companies to find the right developers and for developers to find exciting opportunities.

How does CFD work?

Developers create profiles showcasing their skills, experience, and portfolio. Companies post job opportunities or search for developers based on specific criteria. Our AI-powered matching algorithm then connects the right developers with the right opportunities, ensuring a perfect fit for both parties.

Is CFD free to use?

CFD offers both free and premium tiers. Developers can create basic profiles for free, while premium features like priority matching and advanced profile customization require a subscription. Companies can browse developers for free, but posting jobs and contacting developers directly requires a subscription plan.

How do I create an account?

You can create an account by clicking the "Sign Up" button in the top right corner of the homepage. You can sign up using your email address or through Google authentication. After signing up, you'll need to complete your profile with your skills, experience, and portfolio.

Can I delete my account?

Yes, you can delete your account at any time. Go to your account settings, scroll to the bottom, and click on "Delete Account." Please note that this action is irreversible, and all your data will be permanently removed from our system.

How do I get verified as a developer?

To get verified, complete your profile with accurate information about your skills, experience, and portfolio. Our verification team will review your profile, and you may be asked to complete a technical assessment or provide references. Once verified, your profile will display a verification badge, increasing your visibility to potential employers.

How do I showcase my portfolio?

You can showcase your portfolio by adding links to your GitHub, personal website, or other platforms where your work is displayed. You can also upload screenshots or videos of your projects directly to your CFD profile. Premium members can create detailed case studies for each project, highlighting their specific contributions and the technologies used.

How do I post a job?

To post a job, you need to create a company account and subscribe to one of our company plans. Once subscribed, click on "Post a Job" in your dashboard, fill out the job details including requirements, responsibilities, and compensation, and submit it for review. After approval, your job will be visible to developers matching your criteria.

How does the matching algorithm work?

Our AI-powered matching algorithm analyzes multiple factors including technical skills, experience level, industry expertise, work preferences, and past project success. It goes beyond keyword matching to understand the context of skills and experience, ensuring that you find developers who are not just qualified on paper but are the right fit for your specific project needs.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express), PayPal, and bank transfers. For enterprise plans, we also offer invoice-based payments with net-30 terms. All payments are processed securely through our payment partners, and we never store your full credit card information on our servers.

Can I get a refund?

We offer a 14-day money-back guarantee for all new subscriptions. If you're not satisfied with our service within the first 14 days, you can request a full refund. After this period, refunds are handled on a case-by-case basis. To request a refund, please contact our support team through the contact page or email support@cfd.dev.
